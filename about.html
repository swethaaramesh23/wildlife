Biography

A Life Wild

Stackly was born out of a profound passion for the untamed wild and a desire to document the fleeting moments of nature's majesty. Our journey began over a decade ago, tracking elusive big cats across the sprawling savannas of Africa and the dense jungles of India.

Over the years, our work has evolved from simple observation to a dedicated mission of visual storytelling. We have faced extreme conditions—from blistering heat to freezing storms—all to capture the perfect light and the raw emotion of the wild. Our photographs have been featured in leading conservation magazines and global exhibitions.

Today, Stackly stands as a premier creative agency specializing in wildlife photography. We don't just take pictures; we craft cinematic narratives that highlight the delicate balance of life on Earth.

The Path

Professional Journey

2010 - The Awakening

Purchased my first professional DSLR and embarked on a solo expedition to the Masai Mara, sparking a lifelong obsession with documenting apex predators.

2015 - Global Recognition

Published first major feature in National Geographic and won the 'Emerging Nature Photographer' award for the series on endangered Bengal Tigers.

2019 - The Frozen North

Spent 3 months documenting polar bears in Svalbard under extreme conditions, resulting in the critically acclaimed "Ice Lords" cinematic exhibition.

2023 - Stackly Agency

Founded Stackly to combine premium photography, direct conservation funding efforts, and exclusive immersive expeditions for fellow enthusiasts.
