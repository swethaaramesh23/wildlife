About The Artist

Dedicated to preserving the world's most vulnerable species through visual storytelling.

Stackly was born out of a profound passion for the untamed wild and a desire to document the fleeting moments of nature's majesty. Our journey began over a decade ago, tracking elusive big cats across the sprawling savannas of Africa and the dense jungles of India.

Over the years, our work has evolved from simple observation to a dedicated mission of visual storytelling. We have faced extreme conditions—from blistering heat to freezing storms—all to capture the perfect light and the raw emotion of the wild. Our photographs have been featured in leading conservation magazines and global exhibitions, bringing the beauty of remote ecosystems directly to the public eye.

Today, Stackly stands as a premier creative agency specializing in wildlife photography. We don't just take pictures; we craft cinematic narratives that highlight the delicate balance of life on Earth, hoping to inspire a new generation of conservationists.

Photography Journey

2010 - The Beginning

Purchased my first DSLR and embarked on a solo trip to Kenya, sparking a lifelong obsession with big cats.

2015 - Going Pro

Published first major feature in National Geographic and won the 'Emerging Nature Photographer' award.

2019 - Arctic Expedition

Spent 3 months documenting polar bears in Svalbard, resulting in the critically acclaimed "Ice Lords" exhibition.

2023 - Stackly Launch

Founded Stackly to combine photography, conservation efforts, and immersive expeditions.

Conservation Mission

Photography is more than an art form; it is a tool for change. 10% of all print sales and expedition bookings are directly donated to wildlife conservation funds operating in the regions where these photographs were taken. We believe in leaving the wilderness better than we found it.
